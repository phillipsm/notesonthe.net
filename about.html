Hello. I'm Matt Phillips. notesonthe.net is the space where I share ideas and work.

I spend lots of time thinking about libraries and how to make them better (primarily by creating software) in the Harvard Library Innovation Lab. You'll see lots of things on this site that come from projects and conversations in the Lab.

I hold a Master of Computer Science from Virginia Tech. I love VT and love Blacksburg, VA. Go Hokies!

I used to spend a lot of time riding and racing bicycles. Road bikes primarily, but some mountain biking too. Training and riding hard with friends is the most enjoyable thing I've ever done.
