About

Hello. I'm Matt Phillips.

I like libraries, the interwebs, and bicycles.

I spend the workday thinking about libraries and how to make them better, primarily by creating software, in the Harvard Library Innovation Lab. You'll see plenty of things on this site that come from projects and conversations in the Lab.

I dig computers, especially those on the Internet/the Web. I earned a Master of Science in Computer Science from Virginia Tech. I love VT and Blacksburg, VA.

I used to spend a lot of time riding and racing bicycles. Training and riding hard with friends is the most enjoyable thing I've ever done.

If you are interested in building something cool together or just want to chat about bicycles, find me on Twitter or send me an email.
