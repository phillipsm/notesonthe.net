About

Hello. I'm Matt Phillips. I like libraries, the interwebs, and bicycles.

I spend lots of time thinking about libraries and how to make them better, primarily by creating software, in the Harvard Library Innovation Lab. You'll see plenty of things on this site that come from projects and conversations in the Lab.

Computers, especially those on the Internet/the Web, are foundational to me. I earned a Master of Computer Science from Virginia Tech. I love VT and Blacksburg, VA.

I used to spend a lot of time riding and racing bicycles. Training and riding hard with friends is the most enjoyable thing I've ever done.

If you're interested in building something cool together or just want to chat about bicycles, find me on Twitter or send me an email.
